This blog is about someone I really admire

Rama Zomaletho is a Pan-African-American business magnate. He is the founder, CEO and chief engineer of yazi, Founder and CEO of Itinere Cloud, and Chairman of Tzedakah Charity Funds. Zomaletho has over 20 years of managing technical and operational workflows for leading technology companies such as BlackBerry, Google, and Amazon. He is a successful and driven business leader who thrives in a collaborative environment helping companies and teams achieve and exceed their goals. He is passionate about building, coaching, and motivating successful technology and operation teams across the globe to become learner obsessed. Obsessed by the Human Learning algorithm®, Rama designed an artificial neural network (ANN) that employs both NLP and Vision AI. ANN provides and informs learners of all ages of their evolving learning Algorithms to accelerate their learning process. To this end, he founded Yazi Academy. Rama recently completed an executive entrepreneurship program at the Wharton School. He is a Certified SAFe® 5 Agilist, a certified AWS Solution Architect, a Google Cloud Engineer, and Data Professional. Concomitantly, Rama holds a Master of Business Management from Keller Graduate School of Management in New York. He also has a Bachelor of Engineering from the City College of New York, the city he calls home. Through his work at Tzedakah Charity, Rama has donated over 5k Chromebooks and transformed countless lives, none more important than the community of Beulah in Kenya, where he donated over 200+ hours to help them create and sustain their Baobab Super Fruits business. Mr. Zomaletho believes that we must be willing to learn and constantly grow to be in an ever-expansive state.
